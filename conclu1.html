THE PROBLEM

Large Language Models are incredible, but they are prediction engines, not truth engines. They "hallucinate" facts with absolute certainty, leading to a world where misinformation looks professional and authoritative.

THE IDEA

Fact-checking shouldn't feel like a chore. I wanted to turn critical thinking into a high-octane game. By gamifying the search for glitches, we train our brains to spot patterns of algorithmic overconfidence.

THE IMPACT

This app empowers you to think independently. We want to move users from blind trust to "trust but verify," ensuring that humans remain the ultimate arbiters of reality in an AI-saturated world.

WHAT'S NEXT

This is just Level One. We're building more complex datasets, real-time news scanning, and collaborative "Debunking Raids" to help communities verify the digital world together.
